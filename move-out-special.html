Quick answers

Before you book

How far in advance should I book?

At least 48 hours is ideal, though we'll do our best to accommodate tighter timelines. If you're in a crunch, call us directly at (623) 321-4966 and we'll see what we can do.

Do I need to be there during the clean?

No. Most tenants hand off a key or provide a code and let us work. We'll send you photos when we're done so you have a record before you turn in your keys.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens. Contact us as soon as you know and we'll find another time. The earlier you let us know, the easier it is to move things around.

Is the unit supposed to be empty?

It doesn't have to be completely empty, but the easier it is to access surfaces, the more thorough the clean. If furniture or boxes are still there, let us know when you book so we can set the right expectations.
