Before you book

Is the $150 really flat?

Yes. One cleaner, three hours, $150 — regardless of your home's size or how many rooms you pick. The only thing you choose is where the time goes.

How many areas can I pick?

Three or four works best for a three-hour visit. You can list more, but your cleaner works your top priorities first, so put the most important rooms at the top.

Do you bring supplies?

Yes, everything. If you'd rather we use your own products for sensitivities or specific surfaces, just note it when you book.

What if I'm not happy with an area?

Tell your cleaner before they leave and they'll make it right on the spot. We'd rather fix it there than have you unhappy.

Where do you clean?
