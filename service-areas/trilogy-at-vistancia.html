House Cleaning at Trilogy at Vistancia, Peoria

Trilogy at Vistancia is one of the higher-end active-adult communities in the West Valley — Shea Homes' flagship 55+ property in Peoria, built within the larger Vistancia master plan. Our work here skews toward recurring residential on a weekly or bi-weekly schedule.

Shea Homes' 55+ flagship in Peoria

Trilogy at Vistancia was developed by Shea Homes starting in 2004 and sold out in 2024 with around 3,300 homes. It's the 55+ gated section inside the broader Vistancia master plan in Peoria — the rest of Vistancia is family-friendly and not age-restricted, but Trilogy is. Homes range from about 1,200 to 3,100 square feet, mostly single-story ranch floor plans with the Shea Homes upgrade profile the community is known for.

Trilogy skews newer-retirement and higher-income compared to the original Del Webb communities. The Kiva Club (35,000 sqft of clubhouse, fitness, pools) anchors the community along with the Gary Panks-designed Trilogy Golf Club. Homes have larger lots than the original Sun City and many have golf-course or mountain views.

What gets booked most at Trilogy

Recurring residential, weekly or bi-weekly

The single most common job pattern here. Larger homes than the older Del Webb communities, more upgraded finishes, and clients who tend toward more-frequent recurring schedules. Same crew every visit. Recurring discount applies.

Snowbird seasonal cleans

Trilogy has a snowbird share but it's smaller than at the older Del Webb communities — Trilogy residents are more often full-time or near-full-time. Pre-arrival and pre-departure work still comes up.

Deep cleans before entertaining

Trilogy is a social community — clubs, group gatherings, holiday hosting. We get deep-clean bookings before big entertaining weekends, especially around Thanksgiving, the December holidays, and Easter.

The Shea Homes upgrade profile

Shea Homes built Trilogy with a particular finish profile — upgraded cabinetry, larger kitchen islands, walk-in pantries, oversized master bathrooms with separate tub and shower in most plans, and tile or LVP through most of the home. Keeping these finishes looking new is the recurring cleaning work — proper pH-balanced cleaners on quartz and granite, soft cloths on cabinet faces, grout maintenance on tile, and attention to the larger square footage of master baths.

Most homes have private backyards with patios, often with mountain or golf course views. Patio glass doors and exterior-facing windows pick up more dust than interior rooms — if you want those included in your recurring scope, mention it when you book.

Gate access and logistics

Trilogy has a guard-gated entrance at the main Trilogy Boulevard access. For the first clean, the homeowner needs to add Spotless to the approved-visitor list through the HOA portal or by calling the gate. After that, our recurring crew is on file and gets through without trouble. If you're new to recurring cleaning and not sure how the gate-visitor process works in your specific section of Trilogy, mention it when you book and we'll walk through it.

Trilogy at Vistancia — questions

Frequently asked

Is Trilogy at Vistancia within your service area?

Yes. Trilogy is in Peoria and falls within our standard West Valley coverage. No distance surcharge.

Do I need to add Spotless to my gate access list?

Yes, for the first clean. After your first booking, our recurring crew is on file with the gate and gets through routinely. We can walk you through the visitor-approval process if it's new to you.

Is recurring cleaning common at Trilogy?

Yes — most of our Trilogy work is on weekly or bi-weekly recurring schedules. The homes are large enough and the finishes upgraded enough that consistent professional maintenance keeps everything in great shape and avoids the periodic-deep-clean rhythm.

Can you handle a deep clean before a holiday or large gathering?

Yes. Pre-entertaining deep cleans are a common booking at Trilogy. We can do these as one-time jobs or as a periodic add-on to a recurring schedule. Book at least a week ahead during peak holiday periods (the week of Thanksgiving and late December are our busiest).

Do you serve other parts of Vistancia, not just Trilogy?

Yes — we serve the broader Vistancia master plan as well. The family-friendly sections outside Trilogy have a different cleaning profile (recurring family residential, kids' rooms, more variety in home size) but it's all part of our Peoria coverage.
