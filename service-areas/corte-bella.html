House Cleaning at Corte Bella, Sun City West

Corte Bella is the gated 45+ community inside Sun City West — about 1,650 Santa Barbara-style homes built by Pulte/Del Webb between 2003 and 2007. Our work here is mostly recurring residential and snowbird seasonal cleaning, with a small share of pre-listing deep cleans.

The upscale gated community inside Sun City West

Corte Bella sits on the east side of Sun City West and feels distinct from the surrounding community — it's gated, it's newer, the homes are upscale, and the architecture is intentionally Santa Barbara-style with stucco facades, tile roofs, courtyards, and the kind of detail work (archways, fountains, decorative tile) you don't see in the original Sun City. About 1,650 single-family homes range from around 1,150 to 3,000 square feet, almost all single-story. A semi-private 18-hole golf course (Corte Bella Golf Club) runs through the community.

The age restriction is 45+ rather than the standard 55+ — Corte Bella was designed to attract a slightly younger active-adult buyer when it was originally developed, and that demographic mix is still reflected today. Median age is around 67, with about 2,000 residents total.

Our work at Corte Bella

Recurring residential

The main job pattern. Same crew every visit, weekly or bi-weekly. Recurring discount applies. Corte Bella homes are larger and more upgraded than the surrounding Sun City West, so the cleaning work focuses on maintaining upscale finishes — granite, stainless, tile floors, larger master baths.

Snowbird pre-arrival and pre-departure

Corte Bella has a meaningful snowbird share — pre-arrival in October–November and pre-departure in April–May are common bookings.

Pre-listing deep cleans

Real estate turnover at Corte Bella is steady, and pre-listing deep cleans (in the week before the realtor's photographer arrives) are a recurring booking.

Upscale finishes, golf course dust

Corte Bella was built in the 2003–2007 window with builder-upgrade-heavy specifications. Most homes have granite or solid-surface counters, stainless appliance packages, tile flooring through the main living areas, and larger master bathrooms with separate tub and shower. Recurring cleaning here is about maintaining those finishes — using the right cleaners for granite (no acidic products), keeping stainless streak-free, grout maintenance before discoloration sets in.

The semi-private golf course runs through the middle of the community, so a meaningful share of homes have golf-course adjacency. Course irrigation creates a slight dust profile on patios and back-of-house surfaces. If you're on a recurring schedule and notice your patio gathers dust faster than the rest of the home, mention it and we'll factor it in.

Gate access and HOA logistics

Corte Bella is guard-gated, with the main entrance at the iron gates on Mission Drive. For the first clean, you'll need to add Spotless as an approved visitor through the HOA's gate system — usually a quick call to the guard house or an entry through the homeowner portal. After your first booking, our recurring crew is on file and gets through routinely. Mention it when you book and we'll coordinate.

HOA rules at Corte Bella are mostly exterior-focused (landscaping, paint colors, structural changes) and don't affect interior cleaning.

Corte Bella — questions

Frequently asked

Is Corte Bella part of your service area?

Yes. Corte Bella is a gated community inside Sun City West and is part of our standard West Valley coverage. No distance surcharge.

Do I need to add Spotless to my gate access list?

Yes for the first clean. After your first booking, our recurring crew is on file with the guard house and gets through without trouble. We'll walk you through the process when you book if you're not sure how to add a recurring service vendor.

Is Corte Bella considered Sun City West?

Yes — Corte Bella is inside Sun City West (it shares ZIP 85375). We treat it as its own service area for booking purposes because it's gated with its own access logistics, but the cleaning work is the same as the rest of Sun City West.

Do you handle homes on the golf course?

Yes. The Corte Bella Golf Club runs through the community and many homes are course-adjacent. We're familiar with the extra dust profile on patios and back-of-house surfaces from course irrigation. Mention course adjacency when you book and we'll plan for it.

Can you do a deep clean before listing a Corte Bella home?

Yes. Pre-listing deep cleans are a recurring booking at Corte Bella. We coordinate the timing with the realtor — usually a deep clean 2–3 days before professional photos works well.
