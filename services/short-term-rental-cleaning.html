Turnover cleaning for Airbnbs and vacation rentals across the West Valley. Reliable scheduling, restock checks, and the kind of finish your guests will mention in their review.

What short-term rental cleaning means at Spotless

Short-term rental cleaning is the cleaning between guests. It's faster than a deep clean, more thorough than a standard residential clean, and it has a hard deadline: the next guest is checking in, sometimes the same day.

The economics of short-term rentals depend on this work being done right and done on time. A bad turnover means a bad review, and a bad review means lost bookings for weeks. The cleaning isn't a back-office task — it's the operational backbone of the listing.

Spotless treats it that way. Reliable scheduling. Same crew when possible. Restock checks for the consumables that guests notice. Photo documentation when something needs to be flagged (damage, missing items, anything outside normal wear). And a finish that holds up to a guest walking in with luggage and immediately judging the place.

What's included

Every short-term rental turnover clean covers the standard scope and adds the operational details that make a hosted property work:

Standard turnover scope

  • All linens stripped, washed, and remade (where laundry is on-site)
  • All bathrooms deep-cleaned: tubs, toilets, sinks, mirrors, floors
  • Kitchen deep-cleaned: counters, sink, exterior of appliances, dishwasher run
  • All floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Surfaces dusted and disinfected
  • High-touch points sanitized: switches, handles, remotes
  • Trash emptied, bags replaced

Hosting essentials

  • Restock check: toilet paper, paper towels, dish soap, hand soap, trash bags
  • Coffee, tea, and welcome supplies replenished (if provided)
  • Beds inspected and remade hospitality-style
  • Towels folded and presented
  • Patio and entry tidied
  • Final visual check: lights off, doors locked, thermostat set per host instructions

Reporting

  • Photo documentation of any damage or missing items
  • Restock alerts when consumables are running low
  • Maintenance flags when something needs the host's attention

Our process

Short-term rental cleaning is a relationship, not a one-off. We work with hosts on schedules that match their booking calendar.

Set up the schedule

Tell us your typical booking pattern — same-day turnovers, multi-night gaps, peak season variations. We build a recurring schedule that matches.

Booking sync

You can text us your turnover dates as bookings come in, or share calendar access from Airbnb / Vrbo / your PMS. Whatever's easier on your end.

Turnover day

Same crew when possible. They know the property — where the linens are stored, where the welcome supplies live, what the host wants flagged.

Confirmation

Text confirmation when the unit is ready for the next guest. Photos sent when we flag damage or restock issues.

What it costs

Short-term rental cleaning is priced per turnover, based on property size and bedroom/bathroom count. Hosts with multiple properties or high-frequency turnovers get a volume rate.

Linen laundry on-site is included. Off-site linen service (where we pick up dirty linens, return clean ones) is available as an add-on for hosts who don't have laundry at the property.

Pricing is consistent — same rate per turnover, every time. No peak-season surcharges, no surprise fees. The instant quote tool will give you a per-turnover price; for properties booked at high frequency, contact us directly to set up a host-rate package.

Common situations we handle

Short-term rental cleaning comes with operational variables that other cleaning services don't deal with. The situations we handle most:

Independent host with one or two properties

You self-manage your listing. You're tired of bouncing between unreliable cleaners or doing turnovers yourself between guests. We become the cleaning crew you don't have to think about.

Snowbird rentals in the West Valley

Many West Valley properties — particularly in Surprise, Goodyear, and Litchfield Park — operate as short-term rentals during peak winter months and switch modes off-season. We handle both patterns.

Same-day turnovers with tight windows

Guest checks out at 11 AM, next one checks in at 4 PM. The five-hour window is the operational reality of short-term rental hosting. We staff and schedule for it.

Host with a property manager but no in-house cleaning

Many property managers in the West Valley don't have their own cleaning crew. We work directly with PM companies as an outsourced cleaning partner — billing the PM, communicating with the host through the PM's channels.

Properties needing periodic deep cleans between bookings

Once or twice a year, even a well-maintained short-term rental needs a real deep clean. We schedule those during natural booking gaps — see our deep cleaning service for scope.

Questions about short-term rental cleaning

Frequently asked

Can you do same-day turnovers?

Yes — same-day turnovers are most of what we do for short-term rental clients. The earlier you let us know about a booking, the better we can staff for it. We need at least 24 hours of notice to guarantee a same-day slot.

Do you provide linens?

Generally we work with linens you provide. Linen rental and off-site laundry service are available as add-ons for hosts who don't want to manage that themselves.

What happens if you find damage?

We document with photos and notify you immediately, before the next guest checks in. We don't make judgment calls about what to do — that's the host's decision (claim against the previous guest, repair before next stay, etc.).

Do you restock supplies?

Yes — toilet paper, paper towels, soap, trash bags, and any welcome supplies you provide. We send you a list when something's running low.

Can you sync with my Airbnb calendar?

Yes. You can share your iCal feed with us and we'll pull turnover dates automatically, or you can text us as bookings come in. Either works.

What does it cost per turnover?

Depends on property size — typically less per turnover than the cleaning fee you charge guests, so most hosts net positive on cleaning revenue. Use the instant quote tool to get a per-turnover rate; for high-frequency hosts, contact us about volume pricing.

Do you work with property management companies?

Yes. We bill PM companies directly and coordinate through their preferred communication channels. Contact us to set up a partner account.
