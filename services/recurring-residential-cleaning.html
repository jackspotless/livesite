What recurring cleaning means at Spotless

Recurring cleaning is standard residential cleaning on a regular schedule. Same scope of work, same standard of care — but booked weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly instead of as a one-time service.

The difference isn't the cleaning. The difference is everything around it. Same crew every visit. Predictable schedule. Lower per-visit price than one-time service. And the home never gets to the point where it needs a deep clean to recover, because maintenance keeps it from getting there.

For most West Valley households, this is what house cleaning is supposed to look like. Set it up once, let it run, and stop thinking about it.

What's included

Every recurring visit covers the full standard residential cleaning scope:

Throughout the home

  • Floors vacuumed (carpet, rugs, hard surfaces)
  • Hard floors mopped
  • Surfaces dusted and disinfected
  • High-touch points (switches, handles, remotes) sanitized
  • Trash emptied
  • Mirrors and glass polished
  • Cobwebs removed from accessible corners

Kitchen

  • Counters cleared and disinfected
  • Sink scrubbed
  • Stovetop cleaned
  • Exterior of appliances wiped
  • Cabinet exteriors spot-cleaned
  • Floor swept and mopped

Bathrooms

  • Toilets scrubbed inside and out
  • Showers and tubs scrubbed
  • Sinks and counters cleaned
  • Mirrors polished
  • Fixtures wiped
  • Floors mopped

Bedrooms

  • Beds made (if linens are out)
  • Surfaces dusted
  • Floors vacuumed or mopped
  • Trash emptied

What it costs

Recurring clients get a lower per-visit rate than one-time clients. The exact discount depends on frequency:

  • Weekly: the largest discount per visit. Best for households with kids, pets, allergies, or homes that show wear quickly.
  • Bi-weekly: the most popular cadence. Right balance of cost and maintenance for most West Valley households.
  • Monthly: a smaller per-visit discount than weekly or bi-weekly, but still less than one-time service. Best for smaller households or homes with low daily traffic.

Common situations we handle

Recurring cleaning fits a wide range of households. The situations where it makes the most sense:

Two-income households with kids

You're both working. The kids have practice. The weekend has its own list of obligations. Cleaning the house is the line item that keeps slipping. Bi-weekly recurring service makes that line item disappear.

Pet households

Dogs and cats — particularly in homes with carpet — produce more daily mess than non-pet households. Weekly or bi-weekly cleaning prevents the buildup that requires a deep clean to address.

Empty nesters and retirees

The home is quieter, but the standards are the same. Many of our recurring clients in Surprise and Peoria are retirees who simply prefer to spend their time on something other than housework. Monthly cleaning is usually enough at this stage.

Households where someone has mobility limitations

When physical cleaning isn't an option for the people who live in the home, recurring service isn't a luxury — it's the way the home stays livable. We treat these households with the same seriousness we'd treat our own family's.

Larger homes that are hard to manage solo

4+ bedroom homes in Verrado, Litchfield Park, and across the West Valley take real time to clean properly. Bi-weekly visits with a two-person crew handle in 3-4 hours what would take a homeowner an entire weekend.

Questions about recurring residential cleaning

Frequently asked

Is there a contract?

No. You can cancel anytime. We'd rather earn the next visit than lock you into one.

Will I have the same cleaners every visit?

Yes — that's the whole point. We assign the same crew to recurring clients so they learn your home and your preferences. If your assigned crew is out, we'll let you know in advance and assign a backup.

How much do I save with recurring service?

Recurring rates are typically 10-20% less than one-time service, depending on frequency. Weekly clients get the biggest discount; monthly clients get a smaller one. Use the instant quote tool to compare exact prices.

Can I skip a visit?

Yes. Text us at least 24 hours ahead to skip a scheduled visit. No penalty. We just pick back up at the next regular interval.

What if I want to add a deep clean occasionally?

Common request. Most recurring clients book a deep clean once or twice a year on top of their regular schedule. Coordinate with us a few weeks ahead and we'll fit it in.

Can I switch frequencies?

Yes. Start at one frequency and adjust as you see how it works. Monthly to bi-weekly is the most common upgrade — homes often need more frequent cleaning than people expect, especially with pets or kids.

How does payment work?

Card on file. Charged after each completed visit. You'll get an emailed receipt every time.
