What move-in / move-out cleaning means at Spotless

Move-in and move-out cleaning is the cleaning that happens when a home is empty — between tenants, between owners, or between life chapters. It's not the same as standard residential cleaning and it's not the same as a deep clean, even though it pulls from both.

What makes it different: the home is empty. That means we can clean inside cabinets, inside appliances, every closet, every corner — places that are inaccessible during a normal cleaning. It also means there are no occupants whose schedules we have to work around, so we can take the time to do the job right.

The output is a home that's ready to be photographed for a listing, walked through by a buyer, handed back to a landlord, or moved into without anyone having to wipe down a single surface first.

What's included

Every move-in / move-out clean covers the full home, top to bottom, with attention to the spots that matter when a home is changing hands:

Throughout the home

  • All floors vacuumed and mopped (every room, including closets)
  • All baseboards hand-wiped
  • All vents and air registers cleaned
  • All light fixtures cleaned
  • Ceiling fans dusted and wiped
  • All window sills and tracks cleaned
  • Inside of accessible windows cleaned
  • Door frames, door tops, and doors wiped
  • All light switch plates and outlets wiped
  • Closets vacuumed and shelves wiped

Kitchen — full detail

  • Inside of all cabinets and drawers wiped
  • Inside of oven cleaned
  • Inside of refrigerator and freezer cleaned
  • Inside of microwave deep-cleaned
  • Inside of dishwasher wiped
  • Range hood and filter degreased
  • Backsplash detailed
  • Counters and sinks deep-cleaned
  • Pantry shelves wiped

Bathrooms — full detail

  • Inside of all cabinets and drawers wiped
  • Tile grout scrubbed
  • Shower doors and tracks detailed
  • Tubs and showers deep-cleaned
  • Toilets cleaned inside, outside, and around the base
  • Exhaust fans dusted
  • Mirrors and fixtures polished
  • Floors hand-detailed

Other

  • Garage swept (on request)
  • Patio and entry swept
  • Trash and any remaining debris removed

Our process

Move cleans are scheduled and priced differently than other services because the home is empty, the timeline often coincides with a closing date, and the scope is fixed.

Schedule around the move

Tell us your move-out or closing date when you book. We work backwards from there to schedule the clean for after the home is empty but before keys change hands.

Confirm scope

Most move cleans include the full house. Add-ons like garage cleaning or patio detailing get scoped at booking.

Cleaning day

Crew arrives to an empty home. A typical 3-bedroom move clean takes 5-8 hours. Larger homes or homes in rough shape take longer.

Photos & handover

On request, we send before/after photos when the clean is done — useful documentation for landlord deposit disputes or pre-listing photography.

What it costs

Move-in / move-out cleaning is priced higher than standard cleaning and slightly higher than a deep clean, because the scope includes inside cabinets, inside appliances, and every space in the home.

Pricing is based on home size and the condition the home is in when we arrive. The instant quote tool gives you the price upfront. There are no surprise add-ons on the day of the clean.

For renters: a professional move-out clean typically pays for itself in deposit recovery, especially in West Valley rentals where landlords routinely deduct cleaning fees from deposits. Save the receipt.

For landlords and property managers: ask about volume pricing if you have multiple turnovers. Short-term rental clients get a different pricing structure that's based on per-turnover frequency.

Common situations we handle

Move cleans look similar from the outside but the situation behind them varies a lot. The most common:

Renters trying to recover their full deposit

The most common reason we get booked. Arizona landlords are legally allowed to deduct reasonable cleaning costs from a deposit if a unit isn't left in the condition specified in the lease. A professional move-out clean — with a receipt — usually beats the alternative. We can send before/after photos for documentation.

Sellers preparing a home for listing photos or showings

A clean home photographs better, shows better, and sells faster. If you're listing in Surprise, Goodyear, or any of the West Valley's competitive markets, this is one of the easiest ROI moves you can make before the listing goes live.

Buyers who want a fresh start

You bought the house. The previous owner moved out. Now you have to live in it. A move-in clean before furniture arrives means you start with a clean slate — every cabinet, every closet, every appliance interior, ready to use.

Landlords turning over a unit between tenants

Whether it's a single-family rental in Peoria or a unit in a larger property, turnover speed matters. We schedule around tenant move-out and have the unit ready for showings or move-in within the typical 24-72 hour turnover window.

Estate cleaning after a family member moves or passes

These are sensitive situations and we treat them that way. Once the home is cleared, we clean it to handover-ready condition for sale or transfer. Discreet, professional, no upselling at a hard time.

Questions about move-in / move-out cleaning

Frequently asked

How is move-out cleaning different from regular cleaning?

The home is empty during a move clean, which lets us clean inside every cabinet, every appliance, every closet — places we can't access during a normal cleaning. The scope is also fixed: every room, top to bottom, regardless of what each individual room "needs." For an occupied home, see our residential cleaning or deep cleaning services.

Can you clean before I move in?

Yes. Move-in cleaning is one of our most common bookings — schedule it for after the previous occupant is out and before your furniture arrives.

Will a move-out clean help me get my deposit back?

In most cases, yes. Arizona landlords can deduct reasonable cleaning costs from a deposit if a unit isn't left clean. A professional move-out clean with a receipt is usually less than the cleaning fee a landlord would deduct, and it gives you documentation if there's a dispute. Save the receipt.

How long does a move clean take?

A typical 3-bedroom move clean takes 5-8 hours with a two-person crew. Larger homes or homes that have been heavily used take longer. The instant quote tool factors this into the price.

Do you clean inside the refrigerator and oven?

Yes — both are included in every move-in/move-out clean. For occupied homes, those are deep-clean add-ons.

Can you do same-day or 24-hour turnaround?

Sometimes, depending on schedule. Move cleans booked with less than 48 hours of notice are not guaranteed. Book as far ahead as you can — the closer to your closing or move date, the more important this is.

Do you handle garage and outdoor cleaning?

Garages and patios are add-ons. Yards, landscaping, and exterior pressure-washing are not services Spotless provides.
