Questions

Frequently asked

How much does house cleaning cost in Surprise, AZ?

Pricing depends on your home's size, the type of clean (standard, deep, move-out), and how often we come out. Use our instant quote tool for an exact price — no phone call required, no obligation to book.

Are you insured?

Yes. Spotless is fully insured and bonded, and every cleaner on our crew is individually insured. Proof of insurance is available on request — helpful for landlords, property managers, and HOA-required documentation.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. We arrive with all cleaning products and equipment. If you prefer we use specific products you keep at home (sensitivities, surface concerns, etc.), let us know when you book.

Will I have the same cleaner each visit?

For recurring clients, yes — we assign the same crew so they learn your home and your preferences. For one-time bookings, we send whoever's available that day.

How far in advance should I book?

For one-time and deep cleans, 3-5 days of lead time is ideal, especially on weekends. Recurring clients lock in a regular slot.

What areas do you serve?

Surprise, Peoria, Goodyear, Litchfield Park, Glendale, Buckeye, Verrado, Avondale, Waddell, and El Mirage. If you're nearby and not listed, give us a call — we may still be able to help.
